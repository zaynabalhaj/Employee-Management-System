Welcome to the Employee Management System

Designed to enhance work efficiency and streamline your operations.

  • Assign and track tasks easily.
  • Manage employee profiles and roles.
  • View detailed reports on team performance.
  • Utilize the translation tool for global communication.

User Stories

  • As a manager, I want to match the skills of employees to projects so that I can efficiently assign resources and enhance project outcomes.
  • As a manager, I want to view my supervisees' progress so that I can monitor project timelines and provide necessary support.
  • As an employee, I want to see my own progress so that I can understand my contributions to the project and identify areas for self-improvement.
  • As an employee or manager, I want to be able to translate content to Arabic for better communication among team members who are native Arabic speakers.
